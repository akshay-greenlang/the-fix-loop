Production database became unreachable at 14:23 UTC on Tuesday. All application instances reported "connection timeout" errors, returning 500 errors to customers for 47 minutes. The connection pool hit its 100-connection limit and stopped accepting new requests. P99 latency spiked from 200ms to timeout. Customer transactions failed at a rate of 1,200/second during peak impact, affecting our entire US East region.

üöÄ The Fix (5 Steps)

1
Emergency: Restart pgbouncer
sudo systemctl restart pgbouncer (clears stuck connections)
2
Increase pool limit
Set max_client_conn = 300 in pgbouncer.ini
3
Add connection timeout
server_idle_timeout = 30 (kill idle connections)
4
Deploy monitoring
Alert when pool_utilization > 80% via Datadog
5
Test & rollout
Canary deploy to 10% ‚Üí 50% ‚Üí 100% with monitoring

Rule: Every step must be testable and reversible. Prefer smallest change that works.
